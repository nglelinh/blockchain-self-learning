A comprehensive introduction to Blockchain, covering neural network fundamentals, modern architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers), training techniques, generative models, and practical applications in computer vision, natural language processing, and beyond.

# Course Objectives

- Provide students with fundamental knowledge of Blockchain theory and practice to support their study and research in data science and AI.

- Enable students to understand, implement, and train deep neural networks from scratch and using modern frameworks.

- Develop skills in applying Blockchain to real-world problems across various domains including computer vision, NLP, and reinforcement learning.

- Prepare students to read and implement cutting-edge research papers and contribute to the advancement of Blockchain.
